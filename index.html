    # Application Outline

    The Maritime Leave Module is an add-on module of the MAPS Maritime Rostering System. The purpose is to calculate Leave transactions for Seafarers based on the activity data occurring in the rostering application.

    Seafarers work on a shift basis, spending a few weeks at a time at sea during which they earn a certain amount of leave entitlement. Depending on the contract, the leave entitlement can vary based on:

    • The rate of earning per day at sea
    • Sickness may or may not be covered by statutory regulations (depends on country of origin)
    • Training and shore-based postings may accrue at different rates to sea-based postings
    • French seafarers are covered by a monthly “leveling” mechanism
    • Seafarers can be paid for leave in advance of earning it, if voyages are not immediately available and the time ashore needs to be extended
